HELIX HUNT

About

This game is designed to test your knowledge of DNA replication and transcription. It was created to make learning about genetics fun and interactive.

Creators: Nandipati Lakshmi Navya Sri, Balle Sudheer Naidu

First Level: Find the Complementary Strand

Second Level: Identify Purines and Pyrimidines

Third Level: Count the Hydrogen Bonds

Fourth Level: Predict the Possible Amino Acid Sequence for Query Sequence
